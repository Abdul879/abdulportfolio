Project Overview : Showing Digital Clock Time

Introduction:

In this C program, the digital clock will start with the time 00:00:00. Then it will work like a digital clock where it will show the time with hours, minutes, and second.

1) Header Files:

First of all importing required header files, stdio,time,stdlib,unistd this are the header files


2) Initilize Variables:

Next step is to initilize the variables hours, minutes and seconds is equal to zero(0)


3) Loops:

Here we are using the while loop statment, to make iteration as the condition becomes true.

4) Logics & Conditions:

Increase the Seconds variable with increment operator, put conditon if it equals to 60, then increase minutes and reset sseconds to (0)
Increase the Minutes variable with increment operator, put conditon if it equals to 60, then increase hour and reset sseconds to (0)
increase hours variable and check if it is equal to 24, then rest hours to 0.

5) Conclusion:

A digital clock is an alternative to a traditional analogue clock. This type of clock shows numbers to display the time in a digital format,
